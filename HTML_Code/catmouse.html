Reviews

The Cat and the Mouse brings a familiar rivalry to the stage with charm, wit, and a surprising dose of heart. This delightful production, a whimsical exploration of the timeless battle between predator and prey, offers both laughter and reflection, making it a hit with audiences of all ages.

- The New York Times

While The Cat and the Mouse attempts to reimagine the classic adversarial dynamic between predator and prey, the production struggles to strike a consistent tone, leaving the audience uncertain whether they're watching a children's fable or a deeper allegory about relationships and conflict. Despite some bright moments, the play falters under the weight of its ambition.

- The Washington Post

The Cat and the Mouse offers a lighthearted romp through the well-trodden territory of rivalry and reluctant partnership. While it doesn't reinvent the wheel, the production delivers enough charm, humor, and thoughtful moments to entertain its audience, even if it doesn't always reach its full potential.

- The Irish Times
