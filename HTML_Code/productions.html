Our List of Upcoming and Current performances

The Tragedy of Macbeth

Step into the dark and gripping world of Macbeth, Shakespeare's timeless masterpiece. A tale of power, ambition, and fate, Macbeth unravels the chilling descent of a noble warrior into a tyrannical king consumed by guilt and paranoia.

Merchant of Venice

Follow the trials of Antonio, the generous merchant; Portia, the clever and resourceful heroine; and Shylock, the unforgettable moneylender, as their fates intertwine in a tale of risk, revenge, and redemption. With its sharp wit, heartfelt romance, and poignant themes, The Merchant of Venice continues to resonate across centuries. Don't miss this timeless exploration of what it means to be human.

The Cat and the Mouse

Get ready for a whimsical adventure in The Cat and the Mouse, a delightful new original play from the Cats Eyes Theatre that brings laughter, heart, and surprises to the stage! Follow the unlikely duo of a clever mouse and a curious cat as they navigate a world full of challenges, unexpected friendship, and daring escapades.

Hamilton

Prepare to be blown away by Hamilton, the groundbreaking musical phenomenon that redefines history with electrifying energy! Written by Lin-Manuel Miranda, this Tony Award-winning masterpiece tells the story of Alexander Hamilton, an immigrant who rose from humble beginnings to shape America's future.
