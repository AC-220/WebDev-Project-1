Green River Productions Presents:

Hamilton

Follow the meteoric rise of Alexander Hamilton, an immigrant orphan who became George Washington's right-hand man, the architect of the U.S. financial system, and a fiery revolutionary whose ideas and legacy shaped a nation. Amidst political intrigue, scandal, and rivalry, Hamilton's relentless ambition collides with the ideals of his peers, including his legendary adversary Aaron Burr, the Schuyler sisters, and Thomas Jefferson.

Directed by Joshua Spielberg
