Welcome to the Cats Eyes Theatre Website

The Cats Eyes Theatre is a well-known and beloved theatre located on the corner of West and East street, Cork. It was originally founded in 1870 and has premiered over a thousand productions in its lifetime. It is often referred to as the cultural and artistic hub for the city and attracts all kinds of cultural and arts fans, and has a worldwide appeal.

Unique Features of the Venue

Cats Eyes Theatre's layout and design are as creative as the performances it hosts. The building itself is a work of art, with an interior that melds industrial chic with bohemian charm. Inside, the space is versatile, adaptable for different types of productions—from intimate, small-cast dramas to large-scale musicals. The seating can be reconfigured, allowing for everything from conventional proscenium staging to immersive, 360-degree settings that break down the barrier between actors and audience.

Our Productions

Cats Eyes Theatre is known for curating a diverse lineup of shows. Their season includes a mix of classic plays, modern dramas, musicals, and original works by emerging playwrights. Some highlights of the theatre's programming include:

  • Experimental Productions: The theatre is renowned for its boundary-pushing works, such as interactive performances where audience members become part of the narrative.
  • Reimagined Classics: Cats Eyes breathes new life into Shakespeare, Chekhov, and other legendary playwrights, presenting their work with inventive staging, modern themes, or multimedia elements that appeal to contemporary audiences.
  • Original Works: Committed to supporting new voices, Cats Eyes frequently hosts the world premieres of original plays. These productions often tackle current social issues, offering audiences a fresh perspective on the world around them.

With its unique approach to theatre and dedication to innovative productions, Cats Eyes Theatre has cultivated a dedicated fan base. Audiences range from seasoned theatre aficionados to first-time visitors, and Cats Eyes works hard to create an inclusive, welcoming atmosphere for everyone. Its patrons know that each visit to Cats Eyes will offer something new—whether it's a captivating play, an immersive experience, or an unforgettable musical score.
